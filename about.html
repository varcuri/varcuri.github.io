Math!

As I've said, I'm on journey to get a Master's in computer science, but in order to that. I need to get all my prerequisite math requirement. What I thought was going to be a simple task of enrolling in a Calculus I course turned into a HUGE ordeal. My high school Precalculus course no longer counted, but not only that, my high school Algebra course didn't count either! In order to place into Calculus, I had three weeks to relearn all of Precalulus Algebra and Trigonometry.

In three weeks, I went through a 1000 pages of a textbook and filled two notebooks; it was exhausting. Luckily, I have developed a love for math; I find it fun like a puzzle. Also, I have to shout out Stewart, Redlin, and Watson's Precalculus: Mathematics for Calculus, as that textbook saved my life. If you are going through something similar, I HIGHLY reccommend this textbook. It has all the important concepts in blue boxes. If math comes easy to you, you could just look at the blue boxes for everything you need to know.
